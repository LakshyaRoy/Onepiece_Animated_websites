One Piece, an epic tale.

One Piece, an epic tale of pirates and dreams. Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, search for the ultimate treasure, One Piece, in a world filled with adventure and danger.

Luffy, One Piece's protagonist, is a carefree, rubber-powered pirate captain with a dream to become the Pirate King. His infectious spirit and loyalty inspire his crew on their incredible journey.
